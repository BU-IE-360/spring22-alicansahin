Introduction¶

The main point of this homework is the getting an understanding about the estate market in Turkey. Conclusion¶

The total number of houses that are sold changes over the time in Turkey and generally it shows a decreasing trend. There can be different reasons to this situation. In this analysis, interest rates and cost of living index seems as major reasons. This two reason behave in opposite directions. It means that if one them is incereased, then selling numbers decreases. Additionally, there can also some different reasons, like Covid-19 or exchange rate crisis in Turkey around 2018. These two reasons also effects the willigness of people to buy new houses.
